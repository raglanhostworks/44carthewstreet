Village Living Rural Setting

Set in the heart of Ōkato, our home offers the best of semi-rural living — wide skies, birdsong at dawn, and the kind of peace and quiet that’s increasingly hard to find. Here, you can enjoy space to breathe while still being just a short, scenic commute from the vibrant coastal city of New Plymouth. Weekends are made for exploring the dramatic landscapes of the Taranaki region — from world-class surf breaks along the coast to hiking, mountain biking and fishing adventures on your doorstep. Whether it’s sunrise sessions in the surf, family walks with mountain views, or simply unwinding in total tranquility, this property offers a lifestyle that feels both grounded and free.

What's Not To Like?

Life in Ōkato comes with a rhythm that’s wonderfully calm — but it does mean you’ll swap late-night city buzz for star-filled skies and the sound of the wind in the trees. The short drive into New Plymouth is part of the charm, though it does require a little planning for errands and evenings out. And while the surrounding coastline is famous for its wild beauty and legendary surf, it also brings the occasional dramatic Taranaki weather rolling in from the Tasman. For those seeking fast-paced urban living, this may feel a world away — but for anyone craving space, nature, and a slower, more intentional lifestyle, it’s exactly the point.
